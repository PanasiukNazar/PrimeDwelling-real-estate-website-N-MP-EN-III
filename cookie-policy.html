Cookie Policy - Real Estate Agency Website

On our real estate agency website, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. To enhance the experience of our visitors and clients, we use cookies on our website. This cookie policy aims to explain what cookies are, how we use them on our site, the types of cookies we use, and how you can manage your cookie settings.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, mobile phone, or any other internet-enabled device) when you visit a website. These files contain information that allows the website to remember your activities or preferences over time. Cookies are commonly used to improve website functionality, make them more efficient, and offer a personalized user experience.

There are different types of cookies used for various purposes. Some cookies are essential for the website to function properly, while others track user behavior to improve the website or for advertising purposes.

2. Why do we use Cookies on our Website?

On our real estate agency website, we use cookies for several reasons. Here are some key purposes for using cookies on our site:

  • Personalization: Cookies help us offer personalized property listings, recommendations, and content based on your browsing behavior on our website.
  • Analytics and Performance: We use cookies to collect data on how users interact with our website, helping us improve functionality and design.
  • Advertising: Cookies enable us to show you relevant advertisements related to real estate services and listings that match your interests.
  • Security: Certain cookies are used to ensure the security of our website, such as verifying user identity and preventing fraud.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use various categories of cookies on our website. Below are the main types of cookies we implement:

3.1. Necessary Cookies

Necessary cookies are crucial for our website to function properly. Without these cookies, some parts of the website may be inaccessible or not work correctly. These cookies do not require consent and are automatically enabled when you visit our site.

Examples of necessary cookies we use:

  • Session Cookies: These cookies enable the website to remember your activity during your session, such as keeping you logged in while navigating different pages.
  • Security Cookies: We use these cookies to verify user authorization for access to personal accounts or contact forms.

3.2. Performance Cookies

Performance cookies allow us to analyze how users interact with our website. This information helps us enhance website performance and identify areas that need improvement. Performance cookies collect data anonymously and are not linked to personal information.

Examples of performance cookies we use:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics cookies to gather data about website traffic and user behavior, such as the most visited pages and time spent on the site, to optimize the user experience.

3.3. Functional Cookies

Functional cookies enable the website to remember your preferences, such as language choice or preferred property search filters. These cookies enhance personalization and make your browsing more convenient.

Examples of functional cookies we use:

  • Language Preferences: We use cookies to remember your preferred language, so you donâ€™t have to select it every time you visit.
  • Search Preferences: Cookies save the filters and criteria you select, such as property type or location, to streamline your property search.

3.4. Advertising Cookies

Advertising cookies are used to show you personalized ads based on your browsing behavior. These cookies help us deliver relevant ads about real estate listings and services on our website and partner sites.

Examples of advertising cookies we use:

  • Google Ads: We use Google Ads cookies to display personalized advertisements related to your activity on our site and browsing behavior on other websites.
  • Facebook Pixel: We use Facebook Pixel cookies to show ads about our real estate services through the Facebook platform.

3.5. Social Media Cookies

Social media cookies facilitate the integration of our website with social platforms like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and LinkedIn. These cookies allow sharing of content directly from our website and help measure user engagement with our social media profiles.

4. How to Manage Cookies

You have full control over the cookies stored on your device. You can disable or block cookies at any time by adjusting your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the availability or functionality of some features on our website.

Hereâ€™s how to manage cookies in common browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Go to "Settings" > "Privacy and Security" > "Site Settings" > "Cookies" and adjust your preferences.
